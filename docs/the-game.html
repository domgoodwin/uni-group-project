Find your way through the maze of rooms inside the house to make your escape.

Set in an eerily familiar haunted mansion, you’ll have to face enemies of all kinds before you find the right door. Along the way you’ll encounter challenges, puzzles and even some mini games. It wont be easy, but we’re counting on you to find the way out.

Discover the secrets and wonders inside, if you dare.

Do you have what it takes to survive?


We’ve got you covered! We’re still in development, so things can change, but you’re likely to face monsters of all types, including (but not limited to):

  • Ghosts
  • Mummies
  • Vampires
  • Ogres

Who knows what will be in the next room… But we do suggest you take your steps carefully.
